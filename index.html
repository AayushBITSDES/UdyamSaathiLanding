The Scheme Access Gap

India has 7.34 crore MSMEs and 22+ major government schemes worth ₹30 lakh crore, yet most micro-enterprises never access them.

The Accessibility Challenge

Information Asymmetry

Schemes are hard to discover without knowing specific names or navigating complex portals.

Language Barriers

Government portals primarily in English, excluding millions of regional language speakers.

Process Complexity

Multiple portals with varying requirements and confusing application processes.

Fragmented Ecosystem

No unified discovery system across different government departments and levels.

Current Prototype

Here's what currently works in our early-stage development

RAG-based Scheme Matching

Retrieval-Augmented Generation system that matches user queries with relevant government schemes based on semantic understanding.

Basic Voice Interface

Speech-to-text and text-to-speech capabilities allowing users to interact with the system using natural voice commands.

Multi-turn Conversation

Context-aware dialogue management that maintains conversation history and guides users through scheme discovery process.
